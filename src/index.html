Pig Dice

How to play:

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold".The first player to score 100 or more points wins.

  • Enter each player's name and press "Play!"
  • When it is your turn, click "Roll" to roll your die.
  • Click "Hold" if you would like to hold your current turn score and add it to your total points.
  • Be careful, if you roll a 1 your turn will automatically end and you will receive no points.
